# 30-Scene Korean Tanager Wishlist — Rationale

> Submitted as part of PineSentry-Fire submission to Planet Tanager Open Data Competition 2026.
> If awarded a top-3 prize, these 30 scenes will be released into the Open STAC catalog (CC-BY) for the global research community.

## Strategic intent

PineSentry-Fire validated a Hydraulic Stress Index (HSI) over Korea using EMIT (60 m × 285 bd × 7.4 nm). The 30-scene Tanager wishlist is the **precision upgrade** — same Korean forests, observed at 30 m × 5 nm × 426 bd, enabling:

- Stand-level fire-vulnerability mapping (vs sub-stand on EMIT)
- Detection of foliar nitrogen change ΔN ≥ 0.3 wt% (vs ΔN ≥ 0.6 wt% on EMIT)
- Operational hand-off to 산림청 산불대응센터 + 기상청 AsiaFire

## Six grouped sites (30 scenes)

### A. 광릉 KoFlux super-site (8 scenes)
- GDK (활엽수림) seasonal × 4: spring leaf-out, summer peak, autumn senescence, winter dormancy
- CFK (혼효림) × 4 (same seasons)
- **Downstream**: KoFlux 25-yr EC tower NEE/GPP × Tanager direct trait validation
- **User**: NCAM, KoFlux PI, KMU 산림환경, IPCC AR7 East Asia

### B. 백두대간 conifer ridges (6 scenes)
- 점봉산 (강원), 지리산 (전북·경남), 덕유산 (전북·경남), 설악산 (강원) × seasonal pair (summer + post-frost)
- **Downstream**: 구상나무·가문비 dieback monitoring (climate-driven montane decline)
- **User**: 국립산림과학원 산림병해충부, 국립공원공단

### C. East-coast fire chronosequence (6 scenes)
- 의성 2025-03 (post-fire 1-2 yr), 강릉 2023-04 (post-fire 3-4 yr), 울진 2022-03 (post-fire 4-5 yr)
- Pre/post pair × 3 sites
- **Downstream**: post-fire trait recovery curve (functional vs structural via GEDI)
- **User**: 산림청 산불대응센터, KFRI, IPCC AR7 NH

### D. 동해안 송이 *Pinus densiflora* (4 scenes)
- 봉화·울진 송이 산지 × 4 phenology
- **Downstream**: species-specific spectral library, mycorrhizal NPP
- **User**: 산림과학원, 농촌진흥청 임업연구소

### E. DMZ uncontrolled reference (3 scenes)
- 강원 DMZ 일대 × 3 seasonal
- **Downstream**: 70-yr unmanaged temperate forest baseline (globally unique reference)
- **User**: DMZ research consortium, 환경부, 글로벌 functional diversity 연구

### F. 한라산 elevation gradient (3 scenes)
- 1100, 1500, 1950 m × 1 summer scene each
- **Downstream**: thermal stress vertical gradient, 구상나무 boundary
- **User**: 제주 한라산국립공원, KMA 기후서비스, 국립산림과학원

## Why these and not others

- **Korea = current Tanager 'data desert'** (0 scenes confirmed via STAC walk 2026-04-25)
- East Asian monsoon-margin temperate forests are underrepresented in global hyperspectral atlases
- Each group has a *named downstream user* with operational uptake plan
- Validation infrastructure (KoFlux, NFI, 임상도, 산불 GIS) is already in place
- 30 scenes / 6 groups = balanced across **disturbance gradient** (DMZ → pine fire → managed)

## Acquisition timing preference

- A, D, E: priority Q3 2026 (summer peak NDVI for trait retrieval)
- B, F: Q3 2026 (montane summer max)
- C: pre/post pair preferred (within 2 weeks of pre-fire signal)

If awarded, we will work with Planet to schedule task acquisitions Q3-Q4 2026.
